Who Am I?

My name is Tai, and I am currently a senior at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, where I am pursuing a degree in Management Information Systems with a minor in Computer Science. Over the years, I have developed a strong interest in the intersection of business and technology. I enjoy exploring how information systems can improve decision-making, efficiency, and problem-solving within organizations. At the same time, my background in computer science has given me valuable technical skills that allow me to approach challenges from both a strategic and hands-on perspective. Looking toward the future, I am passionate about building a career where I can apply my skills to make a meaningful impact. My long-term goals are to become either a Full-Stack Developer, contributing to the design and functionality of applications, or a Cybersecurity Analyst, protecting systems from evolving threats.
